ITEM 1. BUSINESS

Alphabet Inc. (the "Company", "we", "us", or "our") is a leading technology company.

representing a 12.7% increase

- Products: $185.8 billion (62% of total revenue)

- Americas: $126.4 billion (42%)

- Greater China: $54.2 billion (18%)
- Rest of Asia Pacific: $45.1 billion (15%)

ITEM 1A. RISK FACTORS
===============================================================================

Investing in our common stock involves significant risks. The following describes the most significant 
factors that could adversely affect our business, financial condition, and results of operations.

Market and Economic Risks

Our business is subject to various market and economic conditions. Economic downturns, inflation, 
changes in consumer spending, and other macroeconomic factors could adversely affect demand for 
our products and services. In fiscal year 2023, we experienced impacts from global supply chain 
disruptions and inflationary pressures.

Competition Risk

We face intense competition in all our business segments. Our competitors include large, 
well-established companies with significant resources. If we are unable to compete effectively, 
our market share and profitability could decline.

Technology and Cybersecurity Risks

Our business relies heavily on complex technology systems. Cybersecurity threats, system failures, 
or data breaches could disrupt our operations and harm our reputation. We invested 
$20.7 billion in research and development during 2023.

ITEM 7. MANAGEMENT'S DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS
===============================================================================

Executive Overview

Fiscal 2023 Results

Total net revenue for fiscal 2023 was $301.0 billion compared to 
$267.0 billion in fiscal 2022, 
an increase of 12.7%. The increase was primarily driven by strong 
performance in our Services segment and increased demand for our flagship products.

Gross Margin

Gross margin for fiscal 2023 was 41.0%, compared to 
40.5% in fiscal 2022. The increase in gross margin was 
primarily due to cost efficiencies and favorable product mix.

Operating Expenses

Research and Development: $20.7 billion (8.0% of revenue)
Selling, General and Administrative: $22.5 billion (8.0% of revenue)

Net Income and Earnings Per Share

Net income for fiscal 2023 was $66.2 billion, or $4.41 per 
diluted share, compared to net income of $61.6 billion, or 
$4.02 per diluted share, in fiscal 2022.

Segment Performance

Products Segment

The Products segment generated revenue of $185.8 billion in fiscal 2023, 
representing 62% of total revenue. Key product categories include:

- Flagship Products: $111.5 billion
- Accessories: $46.4 billion
- Other Products: $27.9 billion

Services Segment

The Services segment generated revenue of $115.2 billion in fiscal 2023, 
representing 38% of total revenue. Services include:

- Subscription Services: $57.6 billion
- Cloud Services: $34.6 billion
- Professional Services: $23.0 billion

NOTE 1 - SUMMARY OF SIGNIFICANT ACCOUNTING POLICIES

Revenue Recognition

We recognize revenue when we transfer promised goods or services to customers in an amount that 
reflects the consideration to which we expect to be entitled. For products, revenue is generally 
recognized at the point in time when control transfers to the customer. For services, revenue 
is recognized over time as services are delivered.
